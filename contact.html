Frequently Asked Questions

Contact us via email or phone to receive membership information and application forms. Membership is open to all interested shooters who meet the basic requirements. You can also use the contact form above to request membership information.

Members must be at least 18 years old (or have parental consent), have a valid firearm license, and agree to follow SAPF rules and safety regulations. Additional requirements may apply depending on the discipline you wish to participate in.

Check the Events page for upcoming competitions. Download the entry form from the Documents page and submit it before the deadline. Entry forms are typically available 4-6 weeks before each event.

SAPF supports both ISSF (International Shooting Sport Federation) and NPA/PPC (National Pistol Association/Practical Pistol Competition) disciplines. Visit our Disciplines page to learn more about each discipline and their specific requirements.

Competition results and national logs are available on our Results & National Logs page. Results are typically posted within 48 hours after the completion of an event. National logs are updated regularly and can be downloaded as PDF files.
