About the South African Pistol Federation

Our Mission

The South African Pistol Federation (SAPF) is dedicated to promoting, developing, and governing competitive pistol shooting across South Africa. We strive to foster excellence, sportsmanship, and safety in all disciplines while providing athletes with opportunities to compete at the highest levels.

Our mission is to create a vibrant shooting sports community that encourages participation from all backgrounds, supports athlete development, and maintains the highest standards of safety and fair play.

Our History

Established to serve the competitive pistol shooting community in South Africa, SAPF has been at the forefront of organizing national championships, maintaining national logs, and representing South African shooters in international competitions.

Over the years, we have built strong relationships with international shooting federations and continue to work towards the growth and recognition of pistol shooting as a premier sport in South Africa.

Our Disciplines

SAPF governs two main competitive disciplines:

ISSF

International Shooting Sport Federation disciplines including Air Pistol, Standard Pistol, Rapid Fire Pistol, and more. ISSF competitions follow international rules and standards.

Learn More

NPA/PPC

National Pistol Association and Police Pistol Combat disciplines. These competitions focus on practical shooting skills and are popular among law enforcement and civilian shooters.
